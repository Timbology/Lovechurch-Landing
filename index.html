ABOUT

A collective of creatives, based out of LOVECHURCH in Bournemouth, on the south coast of England, we’re passionate about seeing the name of Jesus lifted high. Though we come from different backgrounds and have different stories, we share in the story of Jesus, and we’re passionate about sharing this story with others through the art we create.

THE VISION


To create art that expresses worship in fresh ways, that engages everyone and empowers people to encounter Jesus.

Our heart is for our Town. We love Bournemouth and we long to see Bournemouth encounter Jesus. We pray that our art enables the Church to reach out to the lost and the broken, so the whole Town will know the hope we have in Jesus.
